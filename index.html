About Us

Eat With Us is a restaurant dining blog written by Skye, Cassandra, Jaini, and Kristen. These hungry four are on a mission to let their readers know where to dine and where it's worth waiting in line. Eat With Us encompasses all cuisines, from where to find the tenderest steak to the most divine hole-in-the-wall, grab-and-go sub stop. Through travelling across North America, Eat With Us has experienced the good, the bad, and the ugly. Follow along Eat With Us' journey in experiencing the world through the all five senses.
